UXR AI Playbook

AI for Qualitative Analysis

The goal of this page is to inspire you to use AI in your analysis process in ways that consider your strengths as a researcher. This framework will hopefully also challenge the notion that AI cannot form part of a rigorous research approach.

This isn’t a step-by-step or a how-to in the traditional sense. This is what has worked for me, and also what I’m experimenting with.

Researcher's strenghts

  • Prioritizing based on relevance.
  • Understanding user needs and emotions.
  • Understanding business impact.
  • Awareness of biases at a human level.
  • Focused coding of data.

How AI can assist

  • Create awareness of confirmation bias.
  • Speed up data-familiarization.
  • Identify potential gaps the evidene used.
  • Help anticipate stakeholder questions.
  • Provide alternatives for looking at data.
